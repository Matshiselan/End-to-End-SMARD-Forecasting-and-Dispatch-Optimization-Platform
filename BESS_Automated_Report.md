**Commercialization Report for 10 MW / 20 MWh Battery Energy Storage System (BESS)**
=====================================================================================

### 1. Executive Summary

This report presents the commercialization results of a 10 MW / 20 MWh Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) in the Day-Ahead EPEX Spot market for Germany and Luxembourg. The walk-forward backtest covers a 364-day horizon, taking into account an operational constraint of a 5.00 EUR/MWh direct cell degradation penalty. The results demonstrate a significant outperformance of the forecast baseline, with a profit realization ratio of 122.83%. The total realized revenue exceeds the forecast baseline by 19.12%, indicating a strong potential for commercialization.
